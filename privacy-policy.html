Privacy Policy

Social Media Scheduler

Introduction

Social Media Scheduler ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles your information. The App runs entirely on your local device and does not transmit any personal data to us.

Information Storage

We do not collect any of your data. Social Media Scheduler is a locally-run desktop application. All information is stored exclusively on your own device and is never transmitted to us.

The app stores the following information locally on your computer:

  • Account Connections: Social media account identifiers and usernames for connected platforms (TikTok, Facebook, Instagram)
  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens required to post on your behalf
  • Content Data: Posts, drafts, media files, and scheduled content you create within the app
  • App Preferences: Your settings and preferences within the application

We have no access to this data. It exists only on your device.

How the App Uses Your Information

The locally-stored information is used solely by the app on your device to:

  • Authenticate with your social media accounts
  • Schedule and publish posts to your connected accounts
  • Store your drafts and content locally
  • Provide the core functionality of the application

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device in a SQLite database. We do not operate external servers that store your personal information. Your data remains on your computer and is not transmitted to us.

Third-Party Services

The App connects to the following third-party services to provide its functionality:

  • TikTok API: To authenticate and post content to TikTok
  • Facebook/Meta API: To authenticate and post content to Facebook and Instagram

When you connect these services, you are also subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Meta Privacy Policy

TikTok Integration

When you connect your TikTok account, the app requests the following permissions:

  • user.info.basic: To retrieve and display your TikTok username within the app
  • video.upload: To upload videos that you schedule for posting
  • video.publish: To publish scheduled videos to your TikTok account

The app only accesses your TikTok account when you explicitly schedule or publish content. Your TikTok password is never stored â€” only OAuth tokens are used, which you can revoke at any time through TikTok's settings.

Facebook & Instagram Integration

When you connect your Facebook or Instagram account, the app requests the following permissions:

  • pages_show_list: To display your Facebook Pages in the app
  • pages_read_engagement: To read page information
  • pages_manage_posts: To publish posts to your Facebook Pages
  • instagram_basic: To access linked Instagram Business accounts
  • instagram_content_publish: To publish content to Instagram

The app only posts content when you explicitly schedule or publish it. OAuth tokens are stored locally and can be revoked through Facebook's settings at any time.

Data Sharing

Since we never receive your data, we cannot share it. Your data is only transmitted from your device directly to social media platforms when you explicitly choose to post or schedule content.

Data Security

The app implements security measures to protect your locally-stored information:

  • All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers
  • The app uses secure HTTPS connections for all social media API communications
  • OAuth 2.0 with PKCE is used for secure authentication with platforms

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored by the application
  • Delete: Remove your connected accounts and all associated data at any time
  • Disconnect: Revoke the app's access to your social media accounts through each platform's settings

Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  • Remove connected accounts within the app's Settings page
  • Uninstall the application to remove all local data
  • Revoke app access from your social media account settings (TikTok, Facebook, Instagram)

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page.
