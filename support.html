Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my social media accounts?

Go to the Settings page in the app and click "Connect Account" for the platform you want to add. You'll be redirected to authorize the app through the platform's official login.

Why did my scheduled post fail?

Posts can fail for several reasons: expired authentication tokens (try reconnecting your account), content that violates platform guidelines, or temporary API outages. Check the post status in the app for specific error messages.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your computer. We don't have servers that store your personal information. Your posts, drafts, and account tokens are kept in a local database on your device.

How do I disconnect an account?

Go to Settings, find the account you want to remove, and click the disconnect/remove button. You can also revoke access from the social media platform's settings directly.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We use secure OAuth 2.0 authentication with PKCE for all platform connections, and all API communications use HTTPS encryption.

How do I delete all my data?

To completely remove your data: 1) Disconnect all accounts in the app settings, 2) Uninstall the application, 3) Optionally, revoke app access from each social media platform's settings.

What platforms are supported?

Currently, Social Media Scheduler supports TikTok, Facebook Pages, and Instagram Business accounts. More platforms may be added in future updates.
